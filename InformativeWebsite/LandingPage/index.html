Features

A scavenger hunt app designed for learning languages.

Immersive Stories

Teams will choose from a list of stories. They could be investigating a missing person, hunting the local ghost or playing through some other mystery.

Location-Based Progression

Teams will to travel from place to place to uncover the next story text or puzzle, unravelling an adventure bit by bit.

Learn Language

Through reading story text, doing puzzle research and communication within their team the students in a team will learn new language.

How to play

A student's perspective

  • Join Your Team

    Team up with the others, experience outdoor adventures as a team. Or you can play solo like Rambo.

  • Pick An Adventure

    Decide, with your team, on one of many adventures to play.

  • Follow The Clues

    You will need to move to the story points, in the real world, to progress through the game. Read through the adventure prompts to find out where to travel next.

  • Solve The Puzzles

    You may encounter knowledge questions, research tasks and riddles. If you get stuck, don't panic, game master can help you out with hints.

Finding Hope

'May you never find eternal rest until my mother's brooch is returned to me.'

In 1855, wealthy Brisbane butcher, Patrick Mayne hired Wilhelmina (Minnie) Hope as a maid to help his wife Mary who was pregnant at the time.Patrick died in 1865. Mary had an expensive brooch which she promised to her youngest daughter, Mary Emelia Mayne.Mary died in 1899. Reported in the newspaper. The brooch was not found and daughter Mary Emelia accused the maid of stealing the brooch.Minnie was charged but released due to lack of evidence. Minnie ceases working as a maid.Minnie books a voyage to Europe in 1892. Minnie left for Europe aboard the ketch Sylvani with her new found wealth. Sylvani Ketch reported lost at Brisbane, 4 June 1892. Mary Emelia Mayne curses Minnie. Mary Emelia Mayne dies in 1940. Brooch is never returned. Minnie Hope's ghost is often seen wandering the cloister of the University of Queensland.

  • Team Mission: Solve the mystery of UQ's ghost.
